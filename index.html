A Little Bit About Me

Academics

I am a senior majoring in Computer Science at Cornell University, and am graduating in August 2023. Growing up, I never thought I would pursue computer science. I had always been someone oriented to the arts, so what was staring at an IDE for hours straight going to do for me? Luckily, I eventually came to realize the immense creative effort that came with this discipline. Studying at Cornell has introduced me to some immensely cool people - I am reminded of the human power to change the world every day.

Technological Interests

I find software engineering very fascinating, and have accepted a position as a full-time development engineer. I will be working in full stack development. I am also very interested in natural language processing and data science. Above all, I want to learn how to achieve technological excellence while always keeping ethics in mind.

Artistic Interests

For as long as I can remember, I have loved to read, sing and draw. I think that these passions have really improved the way I approach technical problems. They have taught me communication and empathy, and have created an eye for elegant design. Whatever work I do, I strive to incorporate these principles, so I can deliver the best possible product.
